Tulu Language Enrollment Gaps Identified in Udupi Districts

UDUPI — New data obtained via RTI reveals significant disparities in Tulu language education. While enrollment remains high in some sectors, areas like Kundapura show zero formal uptake despite regional cultural roots.

Our research into the Alupa Dynasty and the T-Signal maritime coordinates suggests that preserving the language is not just a cultural choice, but a historical necessity for Tulunadu.
